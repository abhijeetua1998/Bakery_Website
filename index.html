About Mom's Bakery

Welcome to the Moms Bakery - home of the best Mediterranean food in the Metro Atlanta area and beyond. It is authentic, fresh, and delicious. We offer catering services for individuals and businesses.The Moms Bakery is an authentic Lebanese restaurant / store located in Atlanta, Georgia serving Middle Eastern cuisine - with a focus on a traditional Lebanese experience. We have wide range of menu items. Our store also carries products that can help you in preparing your own Mediterranean meals.We value our customers by offering a fast and friendly service. Hospitality is everything to us. Our special coffee is ready for your morning visits, or anytime you need a coffee break.We serve breakfast, lunch and dinner. Our location is easy to access and is incredibly convenient.The atmosphere is casual and family-friendly. Your experience at the Moms Bakery will make you came back for more.Come and visit us today, and bring your family and friends.

Why Chose Us?

Our on-site store carries a wide range of specialty imported products, fresh seasonal produce, fresh bread baked on premises, frozen products, dairy products (labneh and cheese), pastries, desserts, pickles, spices, tobacco and many other items. If you can not find it, let us know and we will try to obtain it for you.We are a family-owned and operated business dedicated to customer satisfaction and hospitality. We will make you feel like family. Our friendly service and fresh ingredients are the best in Metro Atlanta. Our bakery includes an on-site restaurant and a mini grocery store that meets all your needs. We have olives, pita bread, specialty imported products, desserts, tobacco, and many more Mediterranean delights.Our Store carries a variety of products that are hard to find in the area. Some of our products are fresh bread, specialty imported products like hummus and baba ghanouj, pickles, spices, tobacco for the argileh, argileh, tawleh, frozen items, seasonal vegetables, and many more.
